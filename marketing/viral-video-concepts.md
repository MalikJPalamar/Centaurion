# Viral Video Concepts for Centaurion Framework

## Short-Form Videos (15-60 seconds)

### 1. "The AI That Thinks Like You"
- **Hook (0-3s)**: "What if your computer remembered everything you forgot?"
- **Main**: POV-style showing Centaurion organizing notes, catching missed details
- **CTA**: "Link in bio to try it free"
- **Trending**: Trending AI productivity sound, fast cuts, text overlays
- **Audience**: Productivity seekers, entrepreneurs, students

### 2. "Wait, It Remembers What I Forgot?"
- **Hook**: Reaction video style - shock at AI remembering details
- **Main**: Show Centaurion surfacing forgotten meetings, deadlines, ideas
- **CTA**: "This saved my job - link below"
- **Trending**: ADHD/memory joke format, relatable
- **Audience**: Busy professionals, creatives

### 3. "Centaurion vs. My Brain: Round 1"
- **Hook**: Split screen "VS" text
- **Main**: Brain vs AI challenge - who can find info faster?
- **CTA**: "AI wins. Every time."
- **Trending**: VS format, competitive energy
- **Audience**: Tech-savvy, competitive personalities

### 4. "The Quiet Type"
- **Hook**: "For those who think faster than they type..."
- **Main**: ASMR/study-with-me aesthetic, Centaurion doing work in background
- **CTA**: "Quiet productivity. Link in bio."
- **Trending**: Cozy, aesthetic, studytok
- **Audience**: Students, writers, introverts

### 5. "Explain Like I'm 5"
- **Hook**: "Let me explain AI in a way a 5-year-old gets"
- **Main**: Simple animation + narration explaining Centaurion
- **CTA**: "Follow for more"
- **Trending**: ELI5 format, educational content
- **Audience**: Broader mainstream

### 6. "3 AM Ideas"
- **Hook**: "The best ideas come at 3 AM. Centaurion captures them all."
- **Main**: Late night setting, voice notes being captured and organized
- **CTA**: "Never lose an idea again"
- **Trending**: Late night aesthetic, entrepreneur content
- **Audience**: Creatives, entrepreneurs, founders

### 7. "The Co-Founder Who Never Sleeps"
- **Hook**: "Meet my co-founder. She never sleeps."
- **Main**: Day-in-the-life, AI working while human sleeps
- **CTA**: "Get your 24/7 co-founder"
- **Trending**: Day-in-life, startup aesthetic
- **Audience**: Startup founders, solopreneurs
