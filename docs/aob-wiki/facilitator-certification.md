# Facilitator Certification Program

## Overview

The AOB facilitator certification program trains practitioners to deliver Art of Brilliance workshops independently. It is the primary growth lever for the venture — each certified facilitator expands delivery capacity without requiring Malik's direct involvement.

## Program Structure

### Stages

1. **Application** — Candidates apply and are screened for fit
2. **Foundation Training** — Core methodology, facilitation skills, brand alignment
3. **Supervised Delivery** — Candidates deliver workshops with mentor observation
4. **Certification** — Candidates demonstrate competency and receive certification
5. **Ongoing Development** — Continuing education, community, and quality assurance

### Duration

Typical certification path takes 3-6 months from application to certified status, depending on candidate availability and supervised delivery scheduling.

## Quality Assurance

- Standardized workshop playbooks ensure consistency
- Peer observation and feedback loops
- Client satisfaction tracking per facilitator
- Annual recertification to maintain standards

## Pipeline Metrics

| Metric | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| Applications received | Demand signal |
| Conversion to training | Screening effectiveness |
| Completion rate | Program quality |
| Post-certification retention | Long-term value |
| Client satisfaction by facilitator | Delivery quality |

## Scaling Strategy

Increase certification throughput by:
- Automating application processing (CRM workflows)
- Recording training modules for async delivery
- Building a facilitator community for peer support
- Tracking pipeline metrics through Centaurion dashboards
