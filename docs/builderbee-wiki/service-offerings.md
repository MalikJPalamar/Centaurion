# Service Offerings

## Overview

BuilderBee packages AI automation and GoHighLevel implementation into productized service offerings. Clear packages reduce scoping overhead and set client expectations.

## Packages

### Starter — GHL Setup
- **For:** Businesses new to GoHighLevel
- **Includes:** Sub-account setup, basic pipeline, 3 core workflows, training session
- **Timeline:** 2 weeks
- **Best for:** Small businesses wanting to consolidate tools

### Growth — Automation Suite
- **For:** Businesses ready to automate lead nurture and client communication
- **Includes:** Everything in Starter + custom workflows, funnel build, integration setup
- **Timeline:** 4 weeks
- **Best for:** Businesses with established sales process wanting to scale

### Scale — AI Integration
- **For:** Businesses wanting AI-powered automation beyond standard workflows
- **Includes:** Everything in Growth + AI chatbots, intelligent routing, predictive workflows
- **Timeline:** 6-8 weeks
- **Best for:** Businesses ready for advanced automation with AI components

### Retainer — Ongoing Optimization
- **For:** Active clients wanting continuous improvement
- **Includes:** Monthly review, workflow optimization, new automation builds, priority support
- **Timeline:** Ongoing monthly
- **Best for:** Any client post-launch

## Pricing Strategy

- Project-based for Starter/Growth/Scale
- Monthly recurring for Retainer
- Upsell path: Starter → Growth → Scale → Retainer

## Cross-Venture Synergy

BuilderBee's automation patterns are reused for AOB's internal operations. What works for clients gets applied to Malik's own ventures — Centaurion as its own client.
