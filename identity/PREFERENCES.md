# Preferences

## Thinking Style

- **First-principles reasoning.** Don't accept "that's how it's done." Decompose to fundamentals, rebuild from there. The Fitness Equation is a first-principles framing of what makes AI infrastructure worth building.

- **Visual-spatial thinker.** Diagrams, graphs, maps, and spatial metaphors land better than linear text. Knowledge graphs (InfraNodus, Graphiti) are preferred over flat document lists. Architecture diagrams over bullet points.

- **Metaphor-driven understanding.** "The Markov Blanket is the cell membrane of the mind" communicates more than a mathematical definition. Centaurion uses metaphors intentionally: Nova = afferent nervous system, Cortex = prefrontal cortex, Daemon = the Greek daimon.

- **Systems over sequences.** See the whole before the parts. Understand the topology, then zoom into nodes. Gap analysis (what's missing?) is often more valuable than status reporting (what's done?).

## Communication Preferences

- **Concise over comprehensive.** A crisp 3-bullet summary beats a 3-page report. Expand on request, not by default.
- **Show the structure.** Tables, diagrams, and hierarchies over prose. Malik scans structure on a phone screen.
- **Surface the tradeoffs.** Don't present one option as obvious. Show the decision space: "Option A optimizes for X at the cost of Y. Option B does the reverse."
- **Phone-readable formatting.** Short paragraphs. Clear headers. No walls of text. GitHub Issues and Telegram messages are the primary review surfaces.

## Work Preferences

- **Batch, don't interrupt.** Malik sets direction in focused sessions (morning phone review). Agents execute asynchronously. Don't require real-time human input for routine work.
- **Ratings over reports.** A 1-5 rating on yesterday's output is faster and more useful than a written review. Capture ratings, trend them, learn from them.
- **Knowledge should compound.** Every interaction should leave the system smarter. If we researched something, it goes in the wiki. If we learned something, it goes in LEARNING/. If a fact changed, it updates in Graphiti.
