# AOB Operations

## Purpose

Manage day-to-day operations for the Art of Breath (AOB) venture, including CRM management, membership support, facilitator coordination, and content scheduling.

## Systems

- **CRM:** Ontraport for contact management, tagging, and email sequences
- **Community:** Mighty Networks for membership access and community engagement
- **Facilitator Program:** Tracking facilitator certifications, renewals, and support requests

## Procedure

### 1. CRM Health Check
- Review Ontraport contact hygiene: duplicates, bounced emails, stale tags
- Verify active membership tags match Mighty Networks access
- Check email sequence performance (open rates, click rates)

### 2. Facilitator Operations
- Track facilitator certification status and upcoming renewals
- Process new facilitator applications
- Route facilitator support requests to appropriate resources

### 3. Membership Support
- Monitor membership signups and cancellations
- Resolve access issues between Ontraport and Mighty Networks
- Track content engagement metrics

## Example

For example, when a facilitator renewal is approaching: check Ontraport for certification expiry date, send renewal sequence, update Mighty Networks access upon payment confirmation, and log the renewal in the facilitator tracker.

## Routing

Most AOB ops tasks are routine (novelty < 0.3, stakes < 0.4). Exceptions:
- Facilitator disputes or complaints → route to Malik
- Bulk CRM changes affecting > 100 contacts → route to Malik
- Pricing or program structure changes → route to Malik
