# AQAL + ILP Baseline — Light (25 Questions, ~15 min)

> Answer honestly in 1–2 sentences each. No right answers — this calibrates the system to you.
> When a 1–5 scale is requested: **1 = strongly disagree / rarely**, **5 = strongly agree / almost always**.

---

## UL — Interior-Individual (I / subjective)
*Mind, intention, meaning, shadow*

**L1.** In one sentence, what is the single question your mind keeps returning to this quarter?

**L2.** Which of these best describes your dominant cognitive mode right now? (pick one)
a) Systems-seer — I see flows, feedback loops, leverage points
b) Pattern-finder — I connect disparate ideas into new frames
c) Operator — I convert clarity into execution
d) Sense-maker — I feel into what's actually going on before acting

**L3.** Rate 1–5: *I know what I want, even when the environment pushes against it.*

**L4.** What's one belief you've changed your mind about in the last 12 months, and what caused the update?

**L5.** Rate 1–5: *I can sit with not-knowing without needing to act.*

---

## UR — Exterior-Individual (It / objective)
*Body, behavior, skills, routines*

**L6.** On a typical weekday, how many uninterrupted hours of deep work do you get? (number)

**L7.** Rate 1–5: *My energy level matches what the day demands.*

**L8.** Which daily practice is most load-bearing for you right now? (breath / movement / sleep / nutrition / meditation / none)

**L9.** List the top 3 skills you want to develop in the next 90 days.

**L10.** Rate 1–5: *When I commit to an action, I execute it within 24 hours.*

---

## LL — Interior-Collective (We / intersubjective)
*Culture, relationships, team dynamics*

**L11.** Who are the 3 people whose judgment most calibrates yours?

**L12.** Rate 1–5: *The cultures across my three ventures (AOB, BuilderBee, Centaurion) feel coherent with each other.*

**L13.** Which venture currently has the healthiest team culture? (AOB / BuilderBee / Centaurion / N/A)

**L14.** What's one relationship — personal or professional — that is currently a net energy drain and why?

**L15.** Rate 1–5: *I give and receive feedback without activation.*

---

## LR — Exterior-Collective (Its / interobjective)
*Systems, infrastructure, markets, tools*

**L16.** Which of your three ventures has the strongest systems/infrastructure today? (AOB / BuilderBee / Centaurion)

**L17.** Rate 1–5: *I know where every dollar of income came from last month and where it went.*

**L18.** What's the one tool or system whose failure would most disrupt your operations? (name it)

**L19.** Rate 1–5: *My current tech stack compounds — each new tool leverages the previous ones.*

**L20.** If the Centaurion system could automate or surface ONE thing perfectly starting tomorrow, what would it be?

## Part 5 — ILP: Integral Life Practice (5Q)
*What you DO — practices currently active across Wilber's 4 Core Modules*

**L21.** **BODY** — What's your current weekly practice? Name the frequency.
(e.g., "strength 3x, breath 5x, cardio 2x" or "none right now")

**L22.** **MIND** — What are you actively studying / deliberately thinking about this quarter?
(book, framework, research question — be specific)

**L23.** **SPIRIT** — Do you have any contemplative practice (meditation, prayer, silence, nature immersion)? Minutes/day?

**L24.** **SHADOW** — When you notice a strong emotional reaction (anger, envy, contempt, avoidance), what do you do with it?
(a) work with it explicitly (journaling, 3-2-1, coaching) — (b) notice but move on — (c) suppress — (d) don't notice until it surfaces as conflict

**L25.** Rate 1–5: *My current practices are a coherent system, not a grab-bag.*
