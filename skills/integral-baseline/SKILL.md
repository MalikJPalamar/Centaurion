# Integral Baseline — AQAL + ILP Assessment

## Purpose

Establish a calibrated baseline of **who Malik is across all four quadrants of Integral Theory** so the Centaurion routing gate, skills, and agents can tune their confidence intervals, automation bias, and surfacing thresholds to the actual person — not a generic founder persona.

The result becomes `identity/BASELINE-INTEGRAL.md` — a peer file alongside `PURPOSE.md` / `MISSION.md` that all agents load at SENSE step.

## The AQAL Map

| Quadrant | Axis | Content | Centaurion Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| **UL** Interior-Individual | I / subjective | Mind, intention, meaning, shadow | Calibrates confidence-weighting and tone |
| **UR** Exterior-Individual | It / objective | Body, behavior, skills, routines | Calibrates execution bandwidth + automation bias |
| **LL** Interior-Collective | We / intersubjective | Culture, relationships, team dynamics | Calibrates how AI speaks about/to stakeholders |
| **LR** Exterior-Collective | Its / interobjective | Systems, infrastructure, markets, tools | Calibrates which venture/system layer to optimize |

Plus:
- **Lines of Development** (1–10 per line): cognitive, emotional, interpersonal, somatic, spiritual, strategic
- **Center of Gravity Stage** (Wilber altitude): Amber / Orange / Green / Teal / Turquoise
- **ILP Practice Coverage** (Wilber's Integral Life Practice, 2008):
  - 4 Core Modules: **Body · Mind · Spirit · Shadow** (commit to ≥1 practice in each)
  - Auxiliary Modules: Ethics, Relationships, Work, Creativity, Service, Emotions, Communication, Sex, Parenting
  - Principle: *"cross-training for enlightenment"* — the weakest Core module is the leverage point

## Two Depths

- **Light** — 25 questions, 15 minutes. 5 per quadrant + 5 ILP core-module practice questions. Produces a rough AQAL + practice-coverage map for immediate calibration.
- **Deep** — 75 questions, 50 minutes. 15 per quadrant + 15 ILP (core + auxiliary modules) + lines of development + stage indicators. Produces the full `BASELINE-INTEGRAL.md`.

Recommended: do Light first to seed immediate calibration, then schedule Deep within 7 days.

## Files

- `assessment-light.md` — 20-question version
- `assessment-deep.md` — 60-question version + scoring rubric
- `scoring.md` — how to translate answers into routing-gate adjustments
- Output lives at `identity/BASELINE-INTEGRAL.md`

## Routing

Assessment itself is low-novelty, low-stakes, highly reversible → `ai_autonomous` for delivery. The *output* (the baseline itself) is high-novelty and high-stakes (it recalibrates the whole system) → `ai_with_review` — Malik confirms each quadrant before it becomes canonical.
