# AQAL + ILP Baseline — Deep (75 Questions, ~50 min)

> Full Integral calibration: 15 per quadrant + lines of development + stage indicators.
> Answers drive `identity/BASELINE-INTEGRAL.md`, which becomes a permanent calibration layer for every agent's SENSE step.
> Ratings: **1 = strongly disagree / rarely**, **5 = strongly agree / almost always**. Free-text: 1–3 sentences.

---

## Part 1 — UL: Interior-Individual (15Q)
*Mind · Intention · Meaning · Shadow · Identity*

### Cognition & Frame-Setting
**D1.** How would you describe your natural epistemology in one sentence? (e.g., "I trust patterns I can name," "I trust lived evidence over models")
**D2.** Rate 1–5: *I update beliefs quickly when presented with disconfirming evidence.*
**D3.** What's your dominant thinking failure mode? (over-abstraction / premature commitment / analysis paralysis / consensus drift / other)
**D4.** Rate 1–5: *I can hold two contradictory hypotheses in mind without forcing resolution.*

### Motivation & Values
**D5.** Rank these intrinsic drivers from strongest (1) to weakest (5): mastery · autonomy · meaning · status · legacy
**D6.** What is the one thing you would still be doing if no one paid you?
**D7.** Rate 1–5: *My daily actions feel aligned with my stated values.*
**D8.** Describe a recent moment where your values were tested. What did you choose?

### Shadow & Blindspots
**D9.** What feedback do you hear most often that you resist? (and is there truth in it?)
**D10.** Rate 1–5: *I notice when my ego is driving vs. when I'm responding from clarity.*
**D11.** What do you most fear being found out about?

### Meaning & Direction
**D12.** Complete: "If Centaurion succeeds, the thing I'll have proved is ___."
**D13.** Rate 1–5: *I feel my work is contributing to something larger than me.*
**D14.** What would make the next 5 years a failure, even if you hit all revenue goals?
**D15.** Complete: "The world would lose something if I stopped ___."

## Scoring & Output

After submission, Cortex will:

1. Compute per-quadrant means (rating questions)
2. Extract themes from free-text (LLM summary)
3. Identify highest/lowest line of development
4. Estimate stage center-of-gravity
5. **Map ILP practice coverage** — which 4 core modules are active, which auxiliaries, identify missing core modules as priority
6. Write `identity/BASELINE-INTEGRAL.md` with:
   - Per-quadrant scores + narrative synthesis
   - Line-of-development radar chart (ASCII)
   - Stage indication + growing edge
   - **ILP practice map** — Body/Mind/Spirit/Shadow coverage + recommended minimum practice per missing module
   - **Routing-gate adjustments** — 3–5 specific threshold tweaks informed by the baseline
   - **Automation bias map** — which quadrants lean toward "AI proposes" vs "Malik decides"

Baseline refreshes every 90 days or on request.
